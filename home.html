Junior-Enterprises (J.E.) are associations with an economic and educational vocation, non-profit1. Student associations located in higher education institutions, Junior-Enterprises are defined as a vector of employability and student engagement. By allowing them to put into practice the theoretical education they receive, Junior-Enterprises constitute a bridge between studies and business, serving the professional integration of students, Thus, they offer services to companies and allow students to put their knowledge into practice by meeting the needs of various clients. : large groups, mid-sized companies, SMEs, very small businesses, administration or individuals.
